The Each Earth Change Title (ECT) is a Non-Fungible Token representing property ownership in the land, water-rights, tools, plants, animals, required create a specific good or service *and* implicit ownership of all future products created by those Sources.

Both Sources and Products are held under terms of the For Product contract.

ECTs are issued and auctioned to secure the property and promises.
