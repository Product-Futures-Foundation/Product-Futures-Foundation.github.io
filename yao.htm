Yet Another Organization is GNU way to fund, work and govern the production we all need.

The YAO UI offers:
[Buy Future Products]: Supplies the YAO with the money needed to buy land and tools.
[Sell Future Labor]: Supplies the YAO with the work needed to begin production.
[Govern]: Group policy is managed through a shared document.  Every change splits the group.

Any user may:
  0. Ignore changes to the document.
  1. Change the document.
  2. Accept a change from another user.
  3. Subscribe to future changes from another user.
