Yet Another Organization is a GNU way to own and control the production we need.

The YAO UI offers:
[Buy Future Products]: Pay with coin-of-the-realm to fund the land and tools.

[Sell Future Labor]: Pay with future-work by signing a work agreement.

You may:
. Change your own copy of the document.
. Allow reading of your copy of the document.
. Block reading of your copy of the document.
. Allow writing from a list of other users.
. Allow writing from any user.
. Block writing a list of other users.
. Block writing from all other users.
