=== Value Forms
Use Value is the usefulness of a product the end user (consumer).  This is measured by the amount a user is willing to pay for Future Value.

Exchange Value is the difference between Consumer Price and Owner Costs (any type of economic rent, interest bearing debt, profit, usury, etc).  This measures Source missalocation, and is 0 when consumers owns for Future Value.

Future Value is the future productive potential of Sources (land, water, minerals, tools, nature, knowledge, ...) plus Labor.

Labor Value is the productive potential of human effort (work + metawork (automation, permaculture)) applied to Sources.
