    Language Enabling Natural Systems

LENS strives to help groups-of-groups buy and own land to meet their own needs.

Groups, and groups-of-groups can set their own policy by writing documents in this legally-binding prose.

The For Product model uses LENS to describe 3 NFTs: Product Tickets, Source Titles and Work Agreements.
