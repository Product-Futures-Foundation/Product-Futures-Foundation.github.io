Each earthChange Source Title (EST) represents property ownership in real productive assets such as land, tools, water-rights, living things such as plants and animals and implicit ownership of all future Products created by those Sources.

Both Sources and Products are held under terms of the For Product contract.

ESTs are issued and auctioned to secure Sources and Skills.
